Caveats

  • Calendars are political, and each jurisdiction has unique methods to proclaim or repeal.
  • This is only designed to be used for â€œhuman lifeâ€ dates, such as -100y ago and +100y from now. In the future we might add support for sub-day time periods (hours, minutes, â€¦)
  • There is no central standards body, these conventions are known as â€œstreet conventionsâ€, i.e.Â each financial institutions and 3rd party vendor has their own reference implementations. Reliance on self regulatory agency (ISDA/ICMA) guidance and case law / precedents is the norm.
